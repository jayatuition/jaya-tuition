About Jaya Tuition - Our Vision & History

Jaya Tuition was founded in 2018 in Klang, Selangor, with the mission of making high-quality, personalized Physics and Mathematics education accessible to students across Klang and Kuala Lumpur. We recognized that traditional classroom settings often fail to address individual learning gaps, especially in challenging subjects like Physics and Advanced Mathematics.

"We believe that every student has unique potential. Our goal is to unlock that potential through encouragement, rigorous teaching, and a supportive learning environment for SPM, STPM, IGCSE, IB, and competitive exams."

Over the years, we have helped hundreds of students achieve their academic goals in Physics and Mathematics, whether it's acing SPM and STPM examinations, excelling in IGCSE and IB assessments, or gaining entry to top universities through IIT JEE and NEET. We focus on building critical thinking skills and deep conceptual understanding, not just rote memorization.
